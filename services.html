Library Services

Book Borrowing

Our library offers a vast collection of books that can be borrowed by registered students, faculty, and staff. Borrowing periods and renewal options are available.

Reference Services

Our knowledgeable librarians are available to assist you with research, finding academic resources, and using reference materials. You can visit the reference desk for help.

Interlibrary Loan

If we don't have a specific book or resource you need, we can often obtain it for you through interlibrary loan services from other libraries in the network.

Online Resources

Access our extensive collection of digital resources, including e-books, academic journals, databases, and research tools, from anywhere with your library credentials.

Study Spaces

We provide quiet study spaces and group study rooms for students. These spaces are equipped with Wi-Fi and power outlets for your convenience.

Workshops and Events

Participate in library workshops, seminars, and events designed to enhance your research and information literacy skills. Check our event calendar for upcoming activities.
